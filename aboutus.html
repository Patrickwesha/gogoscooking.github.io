How we started:

Gogo's cooking has been making popular Zimbabwean cuisine since 2007. The restaurant was started by two brothers who always loved eating their mom's food growing up. Our goal has always been to offer great homemade food for those willing to try out different cultures' food.

Our signature recipes have been passed down from generation to generation. You can enjoy a selection of fish, beef stew, chicken, fresh greens, homemade desserts, and our daily specials, including everything from classic favorites to modern dishes. We also offer the best-homebrewed maize beer called “whawha”.
